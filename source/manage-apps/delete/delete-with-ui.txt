===============================
Delete an App (App Services UI)
===============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

Prerequisites
-------------
To delete an app, you must be logged in as a 
:atlas:`Project Owner </reference/user-roles/#project-roles>` of the 
`{+atlas+} <https://cloud.mongodb.com/user/register?tck=docs_realm>`_ 
project where you want to delete the app.
You can learn more about creating an |atlas| account in the
`{+atlas-short+} <https://docs.atlas.mongodb.com/tutorial/create-atlas-account>`_ 
documentation.

Procedure
---------

.. procedure::

   .. step:: Navigate to the App Services Tab

      Go to the Project Dashboard where you want to delete the App.
      Select the :guilabel:`App Services` tab.


   .. step:: Delete the App

      To delete an app, click the ``...`` at the upper right corner of the tile
      for the app you want to delete.
      Select :guilabel:`Delete App` from the drop-down menu that appears.

      .. figure:: /images/delete-realm-through-ui.png
       :alt: App tile with Delete App option showing
       :width: 300px

      The page prompts you to confirm that you want to delete this app.
      Click :guilabel:`Delete App`.
